Fairy Tail



Introduction

Lucy Heartfilia, a celestial wizard, sets out to find more celestial keys for her collection and looks forward to join the famous guild, Fairy Tail. During her journey, she meets Natsu Dragneel, a dragon slayer, and Happy. She then found out that Natsu and Happy were looking for a dragon named Igneel. They bid goodbye and suddenly Lucy got caught in an incident and Natsu and Happy saves her. Then Natsu takes her to the guild Fairy Tail and became an official member.
